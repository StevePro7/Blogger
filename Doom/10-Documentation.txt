<u>Documentation</u></br />
The <a href="https://fabiensanglard.net/gebbdoom">Game Engine Black Book</a>: DOOM book is excellent reference material which goes into great depth about the development process of Doom in 1993.  <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Masters_of_Doom">Masters of Doom</a> documents the history of id Software and goes into great depth about development of many id Software franchises leading up-and-including Doom success.
