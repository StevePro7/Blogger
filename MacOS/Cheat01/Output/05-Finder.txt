<u>Finder</u><br />
The <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finder_(software)">Finder</a> is the home base for your Mac: you use the Finder to <a href="https://support.apple.com/en-ie/guide/mac-help/mchlp2605/mac">organize</a> your folders and files on the Mac.  Launch Finder | Settings... Leave all options in Finder Settings but check all checkboxes on Advanced tab:
