As a result, M1 powered MacBooks have provened to be much faster than Windows thus making Apple computers more appealing for <a href="https://medium.com/@abhishek.quicktech/why-companies-run-better-on-a-mac-than-on-windows-61272da87980">companies</a> than ever.  Especially with the rise of <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/apple-m1-m2-chips-what-good-deep-learning-denovo-team-fydrf">A.I.</a> requirements for faster data access.
<br /><br />
The issue now for Windows users is to seamlessly transfer all keyboard shortcuts + muscle memory to Mac!

The <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finder_(software)">Finder</a> is the home base for your Mac: you use the Finder to <a href="https://support.apple.com/en-ie/guide/mac-help/mchlp2605/mac">organize</a> your folders and files on the Mac.  Launch Finder | Settings... Leave all options in Finder Settings but check all checkboxes on Advanced tab:
