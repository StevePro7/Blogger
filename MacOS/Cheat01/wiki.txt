In late 2020, Apple <a href="https://www.apple.com/ie/newsroom/2020/11/apple-unleashes-m1">unleashed</a> its industry-leading performant <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_M1">M1</a> chip designed specifically for the Mac.  As a result, M1 powered MacBooks have provened to be much faster than Windows thus making Apple computers more appealing for <a href="https://medium.com/@abhishek.quicktech/why-companies-run-better-on-a-mac-than-on-windows-61272da87980">companies</a> than ever.  Especially with the rise of <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/apple-m1-m2-chips-what-good-deep-learning-denovo-team-fydrf">A.I.</a> requirements for faster data access.
<br /><br />
The issue now for Windows users is to seamlessly transfer all keyboard shortcuts + muscle memory to Mac!
