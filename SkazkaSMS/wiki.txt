<span style="font-family:verdana;font-size:85%;">
  
<a href="https://www.smspower.org/Homebrew/Skazka-SMS">Skazka</a> is a Slavic RGP Adventure game originally written in BASIC for Sega SC-3000 and posted to SC-3000 Survivors Facebook <a href="https://www.facebook.com/photo/?fbid=3051652211766546&set=gm.4887403341348676">group</a>. The source <a href="https://bit.ly/3tGV9J3">code</a> is now  ported to <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/C_(programming_language)">C</a> using <a href="https://github.com/sverx/devkitSMS">devkitSMS</a> for the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Master_System">Sega Master System</a>.

<u>Instructions</u><br />
Skazka captures the core mechanics of old school RPG experience: Kill monsters | level up | upgrade gear.  There are 5x different enemies in increasing order: Razboynik, Hungry Wolf, Kikimora, Leshy, Baby Yaga.  Defeat final boss Koschey to beat the game!

<u>Hints</u><br />
<ul>
<li>
Try to buy weapon "Sword" or armor "Tegilay" with your initial gold before entering any forest fights.
</li>
<li>
After completing each forest fight, ensure you "Rest" at the main screen to replenish all health points.
</li>
<li>
In the shop when you buy items you already have then will be deducted unconditionally so be aware!
</li>
<li>
Get "+1 Life" just in case you lose a fight or the final boss battle so you do not have to restart game.
</li>
<li>
Maximize HP + XP + get the best weapon "Axe" and best armor "Kolchuga" before fighting the boss.
</li>
<li>
Fight boss when you have > 60 XP because your weapon and armor may be slightly more powerful!
</li>
</ul>
<br />
<u>Cheats</u><br />
<ul>
<li>
On splash screen, hold joystick down and press button 2 to change default back color.
</li>
<li>
On stats screen, move joystick left or right to skip over the flashing arrows indication.
</li>
<li>
On title screen, hold fire2 to skip intro music.  Same applies on boss and over screens.
</li>
<li>
In Easy mode forest screen, press fire2 to "Run away" without risking losing any HPs.
</li>
</ul>

<u>Summary</u><br />
Despite the fact that <a href="https://www.smspower.org/Homebrew/Skazka-SMS">Skazka</a> was a simple text-based RPG adventure with limited game play, the cool aspect was collaborating with original <a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/334334389988950/user/100007653903932">developer</a> and working together to port the game to the Sega Master System.
<br><br>
Skazka was entered in the SMS Power! 2022 <a href="https://www.smspower.org/Competitions/Coding-2022">competition</a> to connect the Sega SC-3000 Facebook <a href="https://www.facebook.com/photo/?fbid=3051652211766546&set=gm.4887403341348676">group</a> and the <a href="https://www.smspower.org">SMS Power!</a> community with the goal to spread Sega 8-bit preservation and fanaticism. That's awesome!
