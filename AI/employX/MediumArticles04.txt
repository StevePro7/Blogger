GPT
Prompt engineering: Crafting the specific request for the current task ("Implement a paginated search endpoint and explain the trade-offs.").

Context engineering: Ensuring the model always has the right background information—project architecture, coding standards, documentation, workflows, and examples—without you having to repeat them.

Harness engineering
The "harness" is the software wrapped around the language model.

Prompt engineering asks: What should I ask the model?
Context engineering asks: What information should the model have?
Harness engineering asks: How should the overall system coordinate the model, tools, workflows, and feedback loops to accomplish the task?
