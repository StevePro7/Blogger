3D City is a simple "Shoot 'em up" video game originally programmed StevePro Studios in New Zealand, in January 1988. The game was written using BASIC programming language built on the Sega SC-3000.

In 2016, 3D City was re-written in C / Z80 to assembler to target the Sega Master System (SMS).

In 2018, 3D City was re-written [again] in XNA to celebrate the 30yr anniversary and subsequently ported to Windows [UWP], Android and iOS.
