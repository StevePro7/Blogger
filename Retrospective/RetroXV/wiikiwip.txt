<u>Architecture</u><br />
<a href="https://www.xcubelabs.com/blog/software-architecture-understanding-styles-and-patterns-for-effective-system-design">Software architecture</a> plays crucial role in designing + developing effective software systems. Understanding various architectural styles + patterns available, you can make informed decisions in architectural endeavors to create scalable, maintainable, and reliable software solutions.  Architectural styles are high-level strategies that provide an abstract framework for a family of systems e.g. Layered, Event-Driven, and <a href="https://www.xcubelabs.com/blog/microservices-architecture-implementing-communication-patterns-and-protocols">Microservices</a>.

<u>Architect Types</u><br />
Software architects, solution architects and enterprise architects all <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/career-technology-software-architect-solution-enterprise-hasan">play a part</a> in designing, developing and implementing solutions that meet the needs of the business but differ with technical, company and IT focus:
<br />
<table width="99%" border="1" offset="2">
<tr>
<td valign="top">&nbsp;<b>Software<br />&nbsp;Architect&nbsp;</b></td>
<td valign="top">&nbsp;A professional who designs and oversees the development of each software system.<br />&nbsp;Ensures that the system meets the needs of the business and is technically feasible.
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign="top">&nbsp;<b>Solution<br />&nbsp;Architect&nbsp;</b></td>
<td valign="top">&nbsp;A professional who designs and implements solutions that meet needs of the business.<br />&nbsp;They have a deep understanding of both the business and the technology landscapes.
</tr>
<tr>
<td valign="top">&nbsp;<b>Enterprise<br />&nbsp;Architect&nbsp;</b></td>
<td valign="top">&nbsp;A professional who designs and oversees the overall IT architecture of an organization.<br />&nbsp;Ensures architecture aligned with business strategy and supports goals and objectives.
</tr>
</table>
