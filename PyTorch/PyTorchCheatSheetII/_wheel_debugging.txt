After building custom wheels let's check the "cubin" support via
cuobjdump


01-Example
unzip -o stevepropytorch3d-0.7.7-cp310-cp310-linux_x86_64.whl '*.so'
cd pytorch3d
cuobjdump --list-elf _C.cpython-310-x86_64-linux-gnu.so
