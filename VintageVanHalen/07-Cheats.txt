<u>Cheats</u><br />
Hack the ROM [above] to show the answers for every quiz during entire game session or alternatively press button <b>C</b> five times on Title screen when prompted to "Press Start" to show the answers to the current quiz!
<br /><br />
Also, on Title screen press + hold joystick down while holding button <b>B</b>.  This will show all the game statistics persisted across all game sessions.  Finally, on Splash screen press and hold button <b>B</b> to reset all game stats.
