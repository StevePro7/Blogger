<u>Implementation</u><br />
<a href="https://www.smspower.org/Homebrew/SimpsonsTrivia-SMS">Simpsons Trivia</a> game has 4x difficulty categories: Easy, Normal, Hard and Pro! plus 4x questions rounds: 5, 10, 25, and 50.  Thus 20x achievements setup: Perfect win for 4x categories * 4x rounds plus "Get between 80-100%" for 4x categories.  Shout out to Bl4h8L4hBl4h setting up 20x achievements as per documentation!
<br /><br />
Also, shout out to BenGhazi explaining how RALibRetro emulator processes achievements e.g. Achievement has 4x important pieces of data stored in RAM: Difficulty [0x00], Selected Rounds [0x05], Correct Answers [0x05] and Screen ID [0x0C].  When all values are aligned on Game Over screen then the achievementis set!
